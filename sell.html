How It Works
Sell your car in 3 easy steps
No middlemen. No surprises. Just a fair price and a smooth experience.
1
Step 1 · Online

Submit Your Car Details

Fill the quick form above or call us directly. Share your car's basic details — brand, model, year, fuel type, and current KM reading. Takes under 2 minutes.

2
Step 2 · Within Hours

Receive a Rough Estimate

Our team reviews your submission and gives you a transparent, no-obligation rough valuation based on current market rates. We call or WhatsApp you — whichever you prefer.

3
Step 3 · At Our Showroom

Visit Us for a Free Inspection

If the estimate works for you, bring your car to our Yelahanka New Town showroom. Our experts do a thorough physical inspection — checking engine, body, tyres, interiors, and service history.

4

Get Your Final Price & Sell

After inspection, we give you our best final offer. No haggling, no pressure. If you're happy — we complete the paperwork and pay you the same day. It's that simple.
